LITTLE HEART HEROES

Little Heart Heroes is a youth-led initiative founded by Yuveer Rajpal to fund treatment for underprivileged children born with Patent Ductus Arteriosus (PDA), a common congenital heart defect. Through a 40-day virtual cyclothon, we raise the funds needed to sponsor their procedures.

WHAT’S PDA?

Patent Ductus Arteriosus is a congenital heart defect affecting over 20,000 newborns in India every year.

MISSION 2025: COLLECT ₹ 10,00,000

Save 10 lives by funding 10 surgeries in 2025.

WHY GET INVOLVED

Left untreated, PDA can become life-threatening. It requires early intervention — a closure procedure costing around ₹1,00,000, which is beyond the reach of many families.

Yuveer Rajpal

I’m Yuveer Rajpal, a Grade X student at The Shri Ram School, Gurgaon. Not long ago, I was diagnosed with Patent Ductus Arteriosus (PDA), a congenital heart defect. Going through it myself showed me how much a single procedure can transform a child’s life — and how many families in India cannot afford one. That is why I started Little Heart Heroes.
